Buyer's Guide:
Microsoft 365 Data Protection
key capabilities to consider when evaluating backup offering

Microsoft 365 underpins key workloads for most organizations, and the high cost of outages, cyberattacks, and data loss have dramatically increased the importance of data protection tools.

The SaaS delivery of Microsoft 365 introduces considerations and challenges that organizations must keep in mind as they evaluate their data protection offerings.

Join this exclusive webinar with S&P Global Market Intelligence Research Director, Henry Baltazar, and Veeam’s VP of Product Strategy, Rick Vanover, where they’ll discuss the key capabilities you need to consider when evaluating backup offerings, and how the evolution of the SaaS market could influence hybrid and multi‑cloud infrastructure decisions in the near future.
